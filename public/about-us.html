About Foodie

Discover the story behind India's favorite food delivery service

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Culinary Excellence

Foodie began in 2018 as a small startup in Mumbai with a simple mission: to connect people with the best local food in their city. Our founder, Raj Sharma, noticed how difficult it was to discover authentic local cuisine beyond the usual restaurant chains.

What started as a passion project with just five restaurants has grown into a nationwide platform featuring over 10,000 eateries. Today, we serve millions of customers across 50+ cities, delivering delicious meals with care and efficiency.

At Foodie, we believe that great food should be accessible to everyone, anytime. Our journey continues as we innovate to bring you the best dining experiences right to your doorstep.

Our Values

Quality First

We partner only with restaurants that meet our strict quality standards. Every dish is prepared with care and delivered fresh to your doorstep.

Fast Delivery

Our efficient delivery network ensures your food arrives hot and fresh. Average delivery time is just 30 minutes in metro areas.

Community Focus

We support local restaurants and communities. A portion of every order goes to feeding underprivileged families through our FoodShare program.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices with biodegradable packaging and optimized delivery routes to reduce our carbon footprint.
